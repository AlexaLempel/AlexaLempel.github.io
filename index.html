About Gomoku

Gomoku, also called Gobang or Five in a Row, is an abstract strategy game played on a 15x15 grid. Players alternate turns placing a stone of their color on an empty intersection. The winner is the first player to form an unbroken chain of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The first player (black) holds a strong advantage; it has been proven that when starting from an empty board, black can always enforce a win if playing optimally. Modern Gomoku pro rules have been instituted to decrease black's advantage. Wikipedia
